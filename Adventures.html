Surfing


Surfing is one of the main attractions of Bentota Beach, both local and foreign tourists. Bentota Beach is also a place for those who are new to practice surfing because of the less strong waves. In times when the wind is a little high, you can also take care of people who come to windsurf at Bentota Beach.


Scuba Diving


Bentota Beach is one of the best beaches I have ever seen, Do believe me dive under that crystal clear water and be living proof of that colorfully wonder. There are lots of colorful fish and lots of corals in different colors. Lots of tourists come to see this place so why are you waiting, come and see the wonders of Mother Nature.

Turtle Hatchery


Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery is a must-see attraction Bentota area. Visitors can learn about how we conserve these beautiful creatures and when they have babies how we release those babies to the sea and more. The most important thing is you can see five species of marine turtles in Sri Lanka in hear.


Toddy tasting


Bentota is also famous for toddy, in the local language it's what is called ra. Ra is an alcoholic drink made from coconut nectar and it is one of the most popular local drinks in Sri Lanka. There are experienced toddy tappers who collect coconut sap and make palm wine using their traditional skills. If you are in Bentota toddy trappers are not a rare site.
