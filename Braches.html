Bentota Branch

About Bentota


Bentota Beach, nestled along the southwestern coastline of Sri Lanka, is a serene haven where the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean meet golden sands. Known for its tranquility and natural beauty, Bentota offers a retreat for those seeking relaxation and rejuvenation. The palm-fringed shoreline offers ample space for sunbathing, while the gentle waves invite visitors to swim and enjoy water sports. The Bentota River adds another layer of charm, where boat rides through mangrove forests and serene backwaters showcase the area's rich biodiversity.





Pasikuda Branch

About Pasikuda


Pasikuda Beach, situated on the eastern coast of Sri Lanka, is a tranquil gem renowned for its pristine shores and calm azure waters. This coastal haven offers a serene escape, perfect for leisurely strolls along its expansive sandy stretch. Renowned for its shallow and gentle waters, Pasikuda is a favorite among families and water enthusiasts.





Nilaveli Branch

About Nilaveli


Nilaveli Beach, nestled along the northeastern coast of Sri Lanka, is a captivating paradise where powdery sands meet the crystal-clear waters of the Indian Ocean. Renowned for its natural beauty and tranquility, Nilaveli offers a serene escape for those seeking respite by the sea. The shallow and inviting waters make it ideal for leisurely swims and water activities, while the palm-fringed shoreline provides a picturesque setting for relaxation
